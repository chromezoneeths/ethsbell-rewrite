About ETHSBell

ETHSBell helps students at Evanston Township High School (ETHS) know when the period ends. It is written in Rust and maintained by codETHS. The code is entirely open source and is published under the MIT License.

You can get our official browser extension on the Chrome Web Store.

You can contact us by opening a GitHub Issue or emailing Willow Carlson-Huber and/or Albert Portnoy.
